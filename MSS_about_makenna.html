About Makenna

Instructor Makenna Mason has been passionately swimming since the age of 6 and has taught and coached for 4+ years. She is certified in CPR, first aid, and AED.

Makenna believes that swimming is an important survival skill that can grow into a fun and competitive hobby! In the fall, she plans to attend Sacramento State to earn her B.S. in Psychology, but will continue to offer swimming lessons to her community.
